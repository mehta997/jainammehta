About Jainam Mehta

I'm a Senior Software Engineer with over 8 years of experience building production-grade digital products across ecommerce, Web3, AI, and backend-heavy platforms.

My expertise spans the full stack: designing microservice architectures, building high-performance backend APIs with Node.js and Spring Boot, creating responsive frontends with React and Next.js, and integrating AI agents and RAG pipelines using LangChain and OpenAI.

I've delivered complete ecommerce platforms on custom stacks and Shopify, and I specialize in system revamps, legacy modernization, and performance optimization.

I work closely with founders and engineering teams, taking ownership of technical decisions and shipping software that delivers real business value. Whether it's a greenfield product, a legacy modernization, or a production crisis — I bring structure, speed, and senior-level judgment.

Based in India, I work with startups, scale-ups, and product teams worldwide to move from idea to scalable production systems.
